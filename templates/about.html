About Us

Our Story

We are brothers from Arizona with a lifelong passion for clothing and music. Since we were young, we've been inspired by the energy and creativity of fashion and electronic music.

As the fashion and electronic music scenes continue to evolve, we're committed to pushing boundaries and keeping things fresh, exciting, and uniquely our own. Whether it's through bold designs, immersive music events, or collaborations with like-minded creatives, we aim to bring a distinctive vibe to everything we do.
