What Can Be Returned

Allowed Returns

  • Items in original condition with tags attached
  • Unused products in original packaging
  • Defective or damaged items
  • Wrong item received
  • Items that don't match description

Not Returnable

  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Used or worn products
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Items returned after 30 days
  • Final sale items (marked as such)

How to Return Items

1

Contact Us

Email us or use our contact form to start your return. Include your order number and reason for return.

2

Get Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.

3

Package & Ship

Pack your items securely in the original packaging and attach the return label.

4

Get Refund

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Information

Credit Card Refunds

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, we'll make it right immediately. Contact us within 7 days of delivery for:

  • Full refund or replacement
  • Prepaid return shipping
  • Priority processing
  • Additional compensation if needed

International Returns

For international orders:

  • 30-day return window still applies
  • Customer responsible for return shipping costs
  • Customs fees and duties are non-refundable
  • Contact us for return authorization first
